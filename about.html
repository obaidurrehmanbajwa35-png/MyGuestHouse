Our Story

Al Bahr, meaning “The Sea” in Arabic, is a sanctuary where Qatar’s coastal heritage meets island serenity.

Rooted in Heritage

Our guest house is inspired by the historic watchtowers that once guarded Qatar's shoreline. We have woven the soul of the coast into every corner, featuring traditional Sadu patterns and locally sourced décor to share the stories we grew up with.

Personal Hospitality

We believe luxury is personal. By remaining an intimate guest house rather than a large resort, we ensure every guest is welcomed as family. Here, your stay is an invitation into our culture.

A Sustainable Promise

The Arabian Gulf is not just our view; it is our responsibility. We operate a plastic-free environment and work with local artisans to honor and protect the coast that sustains us.
